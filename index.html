Modern technology

Solar Irrigation

To supplement the constant supply of water from the river Nyamugasani, we use solar pumps to water our crops. This helps us save both time and money as the solar pumps are cost-efficient and use recyclable solar energy.

01

Modern Ploughing

The traditional methods used in Uganda to plough were human hands with hoes, and for those who can afford they use oxen. We use tractors on our farms because they are much faster and more efficient. They also have lower risk of injuries on the farm. However, we supplement this with the traditional methods where possible so that we give jobs to the locals.
